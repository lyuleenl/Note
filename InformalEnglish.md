# eureka

exclamation

 often humorous

**UK**/juˈriː.kə/ **US**/jʊˈriː.kə/

used to show that you have been successful in something you were trying to do

**（成功做到某事时发出的欢呼）我发现了，我找到了，我成功了**

"Eureka!" she shouted as the engine started.

“我成功了！”随着发动机的启动她欢呼起来。
